About Us

Learn more about Ivory Studios, our journey, and what we aim to create.

Who We Are

Ivory Studios is a Roblox development duo made up of Reiichi and Splinter. We work together to create games, systems, and experiences that combine creativity and technical skill.

Our Story

We have known each other for multiple years and have always shared an interest in creating and developing. Over time, we continued improving our skills and decided to combine our strengths to create Ivory Studios.

What We Do

Reiichi focuses on scripting, systems, and gameplay mechanics, while Splinter focuses on building, environments, and visual design.

Our Goal

Our goal is to create Roblox experiences that players remember. We want to build games with unique ideas, smooth gameplay, and worlds that feel alive.


We are always learning, improving, and taking on new challenges to become better developers and create higher quality projects.

🎮 Creativity

Creating original ideas and unique experiences for players.

⚙️ Quality

Focusing on polished systems, detailed builds, and enjoyable gameplay.

🤝 Teamwork

Combining our skills and working together to bring ideas to life.
