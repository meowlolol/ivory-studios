About Ivory Studios

Ivory Studios was created by Reiichi and Splinter, two developers who have known each other for multiple years. Throughout that time, we have worked together, learned new skills, and built our passion for creating Roblox experiences.


By combining scripting and building, we bring different strengths together to create games, systems, and worlds that we are proud of. Our goal is to continue improving, take on new challenges, and create memorable experiences for players.

What Makes Us Different

Instead of focusing on just one part of development, we work together to handle both the technical and creative sides of a project. This allows us to create more complete experiences with better communication and teamwork.
